About Projects

Hello! My name is Antti.

I am an automation engineer from Seinäjoki . I was born in 1994 , and when it came a time for me to pick a profession, I chose an electrician and worked as one for a year.

After that year working as an electrician was up, I got into Seinäjoki University of Applied Sciences , where I studied to become an automation engineer.

During my studies I started an internship at a company where I first did mechanical engineering, and later transitioned into electrical engineering . After my internship was over, I stayed with the company for 3 years before going back to complete my bachelors degree.

I finally officially got my bachelors degree in automation engineering in 2022, and finished my studies with 3 years of work experience in my field!

What do I do for a living?

My professional career has focused on automation and electrical engineering in an industrial setting.

Main part of my career has been designing all things electricity and automation for my employers projects.

These include things such as:
-Designing and drawing the electrical schematics and cabinet layouts
-Selecting the necessary PLC components for the project
-Writing the process model of the automation system
-Writing the manuals for the end users to use our systems
-Being onsite during the commissioning of our projects.

And much more!

What systems and tools am I familiar with?

For electrical drawings I am most familiar with CADMATIC Electrical , and I have designed with it for 4 years.

From the PLC world I am most familiar with Beckhoff products and TwinCAT , but I have also done projects with Siemens and TIA Portal  and some small test projects with Schneider PLC's and Machine Expert.

Even with some experience in PLC programming I am not a PLC programmer.

I can manage small projects, but whole factories are out of my comfort zone when it comes to programming. Programming PLC's is something I would like to do more and get better at.

Besides the before mentioned career specific tools, I am also tech-savvy and know my way around Windows and Microsoft products, and can usually overcome and find solutions to obstacles regarding technology.

From programming languages I am most proficient in C# , but have done coding in C++ before with success. Little experience in python and java.
