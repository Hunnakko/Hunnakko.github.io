Red Dead Redemption 2 Wanted system overhaul

This mod was created to make the wanted system in Red Dead Redemption 2 better.

The original wanted system in the game is pretty restricted, and it does things that don't really make sense.
Like every guard instantly recognizing the main character, even if you are wearing a mask.
Or if you shoot an NPC in the middle of nowhere, you somehow get a bounty for it.

The project is written in C++, and utilizes RDR2 Native Menu Base from Halen84  for the UI elements.

The project has not been published yet, because there are still features that need adding, and code that needs cleaning.
If you are interested in the mod, send me an email and I can send you the .asi file.

I have played through 80% of the whole game with the mod turned on, and it works fine most of the time.

Main features

Make masks usable

Modify the mask system in the game so that if you are wearing a mask before committing crime, there will be no bounty to the player. The mask will not enable you to commit crimes without bounty forever, and the law will recognize you at some point.

Disable petty cime

When in towns and cities, you often accidentally bump someone with your horse, and suddenly the law wants you dead, or you have a 20 dollar bounty. The mod has a selection to disable most of these crimes.

No witness, no case

If the player is out of any town or city area, they are free to commit any crime, without bounty or mask. As long as you take care of witnesses to your crimes, and don't let them escape, you can commit crime to your heart's content. Letting a witness go doesn't immediately trigger a wanted level, but instead rises the heat and response time of the law. Laying low and not committing crime or leaving witnesses for a few ingame days will lower the heat.

Train robberies

If you are in the wilderness and start a train robbery, only the guards in the train will be alerted. The longer the train stands still, the more suspicious the people at the next station will get, and eventually law will be dispatched to find the missing train. If you work fast, you can get some loot even before the law arrives looking for the train, and wearing a mask prevents a bounty, even if you were caught.
